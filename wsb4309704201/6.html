DeRisk makes insurance work for investors in Africa.

DeRisk seeks to be different from other investment and insurance intermediaries through:

  • DFI insurer focus: we were the first authorized MIGA insurance marketing agent, and have completed more than 25 transactions with MIGA and OPIC 
  • Experience on the ground: we work in the places our clients work
  • Focus on frontier markets: conflict and post-conflict regions, sub-Saharan Africa and poor countries
  • Devotion to the long term: business is only good if society and the environment are sustained
  • Bridging the private-public divide: we make public institutions work for the private sector
  • Independence: we can work with the best insurance and financial institutions

The people behind DeRisk have worked extensively in risk mitigation, developing markets, management consulting, private sector development, development finance and donor institutions, private equity and sustainable investment. DeRisk has on-the-ground experience in 5 continents.

 

Andrew Gaines

 

Andrew’s background is in insurance and management consulting, having worked with clients in over 25 countries. Prior to starting DeRisk in 2006, Andrew worked in the insurance division of Credit Suisse in Switzerland and in the insurance practice at Accenture in the United Kingdom. Andrew holds an MBA from HEC Paris and a BA in Sociology summa cum laude from Pepperdine University, California.


Lisa Curtis


Lisa has a background in corporate strategy, private infrastructure finance, privatization, regulatory governance and private sector development in developing countries. She has worked with Bain & Co, Coopers & Lybrand (now PWC), Mercer Management Consulting, DfID and the World Bank’s Foreign Investment Advisory Service (FIAS). Lisa has an M.Phil and M.St. in Social Anthropology from Wolfson College, Oxford and an MA in English from Exeter College, Oxford. She was awarded a President’s Fellowship in Anthropology from Columbia University.

 

Oliver Karius


Oliver has a background in sustainable and socially responsible investment and corporate sustainability strategy in both developed and developing countries. He is also a partner at LGT Venture Philanthropy Foundation in Switzerland, responsible for the Africa investment portfolio. Previouly, Oliver founded and managed VantagePoint Global, worked for SAM Sustainable Asset Management, the Dow Jones Sustainability Index (DJSI), F&C Investment Management (formerly ISIS) and MunichRe. Oliver holds an MSc in Global Environmental Change and Policy, Imperial College, UK and MSc (Dipl. Biol.) from Ludwig Maximilians University, Germany.
